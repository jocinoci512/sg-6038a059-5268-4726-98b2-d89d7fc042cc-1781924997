## Vision
Transform the existing website into a premium, authoritative blockchain investigation and digital asset recovery platform named **Cipherstracer**. The objective is to make the brand significantly more modern, authoritative, and trustworthy while strictly preserving the existing layout, structure, navigation, design flow, and user experience.

## Design
- **Theme**: Premium Cybersecurity & Blockchain Intelligence.
- **Colors**: Maintain existing dark slate, blue, and green functional colors, but ensure consistent application.
- **Typography**: Professional, enterprise-grade sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Roboto, utilizing existing Tailwind setup) with improved visual hierarchy, spacing, and consistency.
- **Layout**: Strictly maintain existing component structure, grid systems, and page flow. Do not rebuild pages; only enhance their content and styling details (spacing, icon quality).
- **Triggers**: Security/Enterprise software. Clean, trustworthy, and highly professional.

## Features
- Complete replacement of all old branding (CipherTrace) with **Cipherstracer**.
- Global update of contact info: Email (`support@cipherstracer.com`), Phone (`+1 (940) 560-9662`), and Domain (`https://cipherstracer.com`).
- Upgraded, enterprise-grade copywriting across all pages (Home, About, Services, Contact, FAQ) avoiding generic AI language.
- Enhanced trust signals (certifications, compliance, data security statements).
- Comprehensive SEO optimization (metadata, open graph, structured data) targeting specific blockchain investigation keywords.
- Security and performance optimizations (minification, lazy loading, secure headers).
